Number Guesser

Instructions:

  • Goal: To correctly guess the random number between 1 and 100
  • Player gets 10 attempts to guess the random number
  • A player enters a guess by typing a number in the input field and clicking submit
  • The computer will tell the player if the guess is too high, too low, or correct!
  • There will be a play again button if the user solves the number or maxes out guess attempts.
